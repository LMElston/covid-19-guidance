COVID-19 vaccines are safe

Canada's vaccine approval process

Health Canada has a rigorous and independent scientific review system in place to ensure vaccines are safe and effective. We will only authorize a vaccine if its benefits clearly outweigh its risks.

Regulating vaccines process

While the government is making every effort to expedite COVID-19 vaccine development, safety and efficacy will not be compromised. COVID-19 vaccines are subject to the same independent, rigorous scientific reviews, quality standards, testing and post-market surveillance as all other vaccines approved for use in Canada.

How COVID-19 vaccines work

There are 2 main types of vaccines: viral vector-based and mRNA vaccines.

Currently, the approved vaccines are of the mRNA type.
