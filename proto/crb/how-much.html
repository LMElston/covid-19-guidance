How much you can get

The Canada Recovery Benefit (CRB) provides benefit payments for each 2-week period you apply for between September 27, 2020 and September 25, 2021. The CRA withholds a 10% tax at source on each payment you receive.

If you’re eligible, the CRB is available for a maximum of 25 periods (50 weeks). You can only apply for periods that are open for application.

  • If you applied for a period before July 18, 2021 – You may receive $1,000 ($900 after taxes withheld) for each 2-week period until you reach 21 periods (42 weeks).

    After 21 periods, your payments reduces to $600 ($540 after taxes withheld) for each 2-week period until you reach the maximum of 25 periods (50 weeks) or the CRB ends.

  • If the first period you apply for starts after July 18, 2021 – You may receive $600 ($540 after taxes withheld) until the CRB ends.

Your CRB amounts may change after July 18, 2021

You will receive the reduced amount

You may receive $600 ($540 after taxes withheld) for a 2-week period until the CRB ends (period 26).

Apply for your retroactive periods first to get the full amount

If you are eligible and apply for a period before July 18, 2021, you may receive $1,000 ($900 after taxes withheld) for each 2-week period until the CRB ends (period 26).

Be sure to apply for any open retroactive periods first before you apply for the current period.

If the first period you apply for starts after July 18, 2021, you may receive $600 ($540 after taxes withheld) for the 2-week period.

You may receive the full amount for the first 21 periods

You may receive $1,000 ($900 after taxes withheld) for a 2-week period until you reach 21 periods (42 weeks).

After 21 periods, your CRB payments reduces to $600 ($540 after taxes withheld) until you reach the maximum of 25 periods.

You will receive the reduced amount

You may receive $600 ($540 after taxes withheld) for a 2-week period until either:

  • you reach the maximum of 25 periods (50 weeks)
  • you apply for the last CRB period (period 26)

You've reached the benefit limit

You've reached the maximum 25 periods (50 weeks) for the CRB. You are not eligible to receive any more CRB payments.
