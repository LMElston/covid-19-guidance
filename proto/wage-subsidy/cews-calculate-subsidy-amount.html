Calculate your amount

Use the online calculator or downloadable spreadsheet before you apply to find the amount of CEWS or CRHP you can claim.

  1. Enter your revenue and employee pay information into the calculator
  2. For claim periods 17 to 20, the calculator will show you whether the CEWS or CRHP amount is higher, so you will know which subsidy to apply for
  3. Print and save the numbers you'll need to enter when you apply
  4. Apply for either CEWS or CRHP online through My Business Account, Represent a Client, or the Web Forms application using the information you printed
