Subvention salariale d'urgence du Canada (SSUC)

As a Canadian employer whose business has been affected by COVID-19, you may be eligible for a subsidy of 75% of employee wages for up to 12 weeks, retroactive to March 15, 2020. [Do we mention that this replaces the previous 10% temp subsidy? Link to how this may affect you?]

This wage subsidy will enable you to re-hire workers previously laid off as a result of COVID-19, help prevent further job losses, and better position you to resume normal operations following the crisis.
