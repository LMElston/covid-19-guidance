Comment faire une demande

You will be able to apply for the CEWS through the Canada Revenue Agency’s My Business Account beginning April 27, 2020.

Get ready to apply

  1. Register now for Canada Revenue Agency’s My Business Account if you are not already signed up. This will ensure that you can apply as soon as applications open.
  2. Make sure your business details and direct deposit information are up to date. This will ensure that any payments to you will be processed quickly and easily.

    Sign in to My Business Account

  3. Before you apply, you will also need to calculate the estimated subsidy for your business.

Incorrect or fraudulent claims

If you do not meet the CEWS eligibility requirements for a period, you will be required to repay any amounts you received for that period.

Penalties may apply in cases of fraudulent claims. The penalties may include fines or even imprisonment.

If you artificially reduce revenue for the purpose of claiming the CEWS you will be required to repay any CEWS payments you received plus a penalty equal to 25 per cent of the total value.

Keep your records

You must keep records demonstrating your reduction in revenues and remuneration paid to employees.
