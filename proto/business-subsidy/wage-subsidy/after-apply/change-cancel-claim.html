Claim period deadlines

There is a deadline to:

  • apply for each claim period, or
  • increase the amount of your previous claim

The deadline is 180 days after the end of the claim period.

Note: For CEWS periods 1 to 5, the deadline was February 1, 2021.

There is no deadline to cancel or reduce the amount of a previous claim.

What to do if you missed a claim period deadline

You must make every attempt to apply, or make changes to increase the amount of your previous claim, before that claim period’s deadline. After the deadline has passed, we will only consider your late application or request to increase your previous claim amount under certain circumstances:

  • Situations where we may consider a late application
  • Situations where we may consider a late request to increase the amount of your previous claim

If one or more of the situations apply to you, you must also get permission and submit your late application or late request to increase the amount of your previous claim within 30 calendar days following the claim period’s deadline.

You must request permission to apply or increase your previous claim after the claim period deadline.

Contact CRA’s Business Enquiries phone line to see if you meet the necessary conditions to submit your late request to apply or change your application to increase your previous claim after the claim period deadline.

If you meet the criteria to submit a late request, you will then be able to apply or request to increase your previous claim using the regular methods:

  • How to apply
  • Change a claim

After you submit your late request, CRA will review it and notify you when we have accepted, adjusted, or denied your claim.

If you do not meet the criteria to submit a late request, you may ask for a further review by logging into My Business Account and choosing “Register a formal dispute”.

Change or cancel a THRP or HHBRP claim

Making changes to or canceling a THRP or HHBRP claim online is not yet available.

Your current options for making changes to or canceling your THRP or HHBRP claim:

Options for making changes to or canceling your THRP or HHBRP claim
Change What to do
Change claim to apply through a different program Submit a claim for the new wage subsidy for the same claim period. Your old application will be replaced when your new claim is submitted and you will receive the net difference between the two subsidies.
Return an overpayment If you have claimed a higher amount of wage subsidy through the TRHP or HHBRP than you are entitled to, any excess amount may be subject to interest. Even though you are not yet able to cancel or reduce your claim online, you can return the overpayment amount now to reduce interest charges.

Change a CEWS or CRHP claim

To change a previous CEWS or CRHP application you submitted, go back into the CEWS or CRHP application under the Payroll menu of:

  • My Business Account, or
  • Represent a Client

If you applied using the Web Forms application and need to change your claim, you may call CRA’s business enquiries phone number.

You will need to make sure you continue to meet eligibility requirements for any updated claims, and keep records supporting your claim adjustment.

When completing the adjustment, you will need:

  • your payroll program account number (123456789RP0001, for example)
  • to know which claim period you are adjusting
  • all of the information necessary to change the amount on each line(s) you would like to adjust

Representatives who are changing a claim on behalf of an employer must be authorized at level 2 or 3. You will need to have an updated Attestation for owner/managers and/or senior employees to support the amended application.

Switching your claim

If claimed the wrong subsidy for a past period and need to apply for a different one, you must cancel your previous claim for that period and then make your new claim.

For example, if you claimed the CRHP in period 22 and realize before the deadline that the THRP would have resulted in a higher wage subsidy, you must cancel your CRHP claim for period 22 and then submit a THRP claim for period 22.

Cancel a CEWS or CRHP claim

To cancel an application, follow the same steps as you would to change an application.

Cancel a CEWS claim

Change the amounts on lines C, D, E, F, and G to zero to cancel it. Do not change lines c0, c1, c2 or c3.

Cancel a CRHP claim

Change the amounts on lines 400, 410, 420, 430, 500, and 510 to zero to cancel it.

You will need to return any wage subsidy you have already received if you cancel your application.
