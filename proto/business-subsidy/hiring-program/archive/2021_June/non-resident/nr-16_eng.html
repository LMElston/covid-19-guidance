The non-resident tax account has been successfully created. An NR75, Non-Resident Tax Remitter Registration Form, will be sent to the account owner at the address for this account. It will include information about the account and remittance voucher.

The account owner can authorize a representative for this non-resident tax account by submitting a Form NR95, Authorizing or Cancelling a Representative for a Non-Resident Tax Account. For contact information, go to International tax and non-resident enquiries.

For information on the withholding, remitting and reporting of Part XIII tax, consult the guide T4061, NR4 – Non-Resident Tax Withholding, Remitting, and Reporting.

Remittances of non-resident tax are due by the 15th day of the month following the month in which the Canadian-sourced income was paid or credited to the non-resident.
