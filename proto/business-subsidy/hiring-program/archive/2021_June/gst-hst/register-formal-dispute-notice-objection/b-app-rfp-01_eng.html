The "Register a Formal Dispute" service allows you to electronically file a Notice of Objection for an assessment, reassessment, determination or redetermination directly to the Canada Revenue Agency's Appeals Division for review and validation.

Learn more about registering a formal dispute

Did you forget to claim input tax credits (ITCs) on a previous GST/HST return and you want to submit information to the CRA relating to those ITCs?

Rather than filing an objection to claim an ITC, you may be able to include any missed ITCs on your next GST/HST return. In most cases, you have up to four years to claim your ITCs. To find out more about ITCs, go to Input Tax Credits.

Did you overlook an item or make an error when you filed your GST/HST return or rebate and now have additional information to submit to the CRA?

You may be able to correct your error or include new information for a previously filed return or rebate. Select the Adjust a Return option in My Business Account. Choose from a list of periods that are eligible for electronic adjustment and enter the adjusted amounts.

Time limit

  • The time limit for registering a formal dispute is 90 days after the day we mailed the notice of (re)assessment or the notice of (re)determination.
