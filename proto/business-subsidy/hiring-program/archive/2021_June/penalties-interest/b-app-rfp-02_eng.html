Supporting document information

CRA delay or error

If requesting based on CRA delay or error, your request should explain the details and timelines of the delay or error caused by the CRA. We will contact you if specific documents are needed.

Financial hardship or inability to pay

We require current financial information to conduct a thorough review based on financial hardship or inability to pay. To complete your request:

  • Fill out Form RC376, Taxpayer Relief Request – Statement of Income and Expenses and Assets and Liabilities for Individuals, to state your financial situation or provide a written statement.

    • And submit:
  • the applicable documentation listed on the RC376 form to support your financial disclosure.
Natural or human-made disaster

If requesting relief based on natural or human-made disasters, submit the following information:

  • police/fire report
  • insurance statements
  • any other documents to support your claim
Death

If requesting relief based on death, submit the following information:

  • death certificate
  • any other documents to support your claim
Serious accident

If requesting relief based on serious accident, submit the following information:

  • insurance report
  • police report
  • doctor’s certificate for any dates you may have been hospitalized as a reason of the accident
  • any other documents to support your claim
Serious illness, emotional distress or mental illness

If requesting relief based on serious illness, emotional distress or mental distress, submit the following information:

  • doctor’s certificate or letter indicating the type of illness, length of treatment (onset of illness and the date or expected date of recovery), hospital dates, if they apply, how your condition prevented you from meeting your tax obligations
  • any other documents to support your claim
Civil disturbance or disruptions in services

Supporting documentation is not usually required as such events are public knowledge. We will contact you if needed. We will contact you if specific documents are needed.

Other circumstances

Any relevant documents that support your request for relief from penalties and interest.
