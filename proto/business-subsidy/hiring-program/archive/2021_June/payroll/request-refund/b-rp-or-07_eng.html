Account balance and activities:

The Transfer payment service lets you move amounts from one interim period to another or pay an amount owing. If you have an amount to transfer, select the "Transfer payment" option under Account balance and activities. Confirm and submit the transfer details. You will see the updated balance and interest amounts right away.

Enquiries service:

Transfer of a credit lets you move a credit that has been applied to an assessed period. To request a transfer, under Enquiries service, select the "Transfer of a credit" form. Request a payment search lets you ask the Canada Revenue Agency to search for a payment you made but has not been applied to your account. Under Enquiries service, select the "Request a payment search" form. Both forms are processed by the Canada Revenue Agency.
