Before you start

Before you fill out the application form:
  • Calculate your subsidy amount using the online calculator.
  • Use the CEWS application guide for help filling out your application.
  • Remember that the rules for calculating the CEWS can be different for each claim period.
  • Sign up for direct deposit to receive your payments faster – usually within 3 to 5 business days.
