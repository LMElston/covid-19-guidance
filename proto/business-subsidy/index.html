Managing your business during COVID-19

Wage and rent subsidies

As a Canadian business, charity or non-profit affected by the COVID-19 pandemic, you may be eligible to apply for one or more of the subsidies through the qualifying programs.

Determine your eligibility

Subsidies

Wage subsidy

Cover part of your employee wages based on your drop in revenue during the COVID-19 pandemic

Rent subsidy

Cover a part of your rent and mortgage payments based on your drop in revenue during the COVID-19 pandemic

Qualifying programs

Canada Recovery Hiring Program (CRHP)

Cover increases in pay such as for new hires and rehires as a business resumes and the economy recovers

Tourism and Hospitality Recovery Program (THRP)

Targeted wage and rent subsidy support for organizations in the tourism and hospitality industry

Hardest-Hit Business Recovery Program (HHBRP)

Support for hard-hit organizations that do not qualify for the Tourism and Hospitality Recovery Program that have been deeply affected since the start of the pandemic

Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS)

Wage subsidy for all employers from March 15th to October 23rd 2021

Canada Emergency Rent Subsidy (CERS)

Rent subsidy for all qualifying businesses, charities and non-profits from March 15th to October 23rd 2021
