Eligibility criteria

To claim the actual expenses you paid for working from your home, you must meet all of the following conditions:

    • or

    Note: This does not have to be part of your employment contract and may be a written or verbal agreement.

    • Types of work spaces

      Refer to the COVID-19 examples

      or

    • What is a Form T2200S

      Form T2200S - Declaration of Conditions of Employment for Working at Home During COVID-19 is a shorter version of Form T2200 that your employer will use if you worked from home in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic and choose to use the Detailed method to calculate your home office expenses. Your employer fills out this form upon request to certify that you worked from home in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic and were required to pay for some or all of your home office expenses used directly in your work while carrying out your duties of employment during that period

      or

    • What is a Form T2200

      Form T2200 - Declaration of Conditions of Employment is a form your employer fills out to certify the conditions of employment and were required to pay for employment expenses

      Note: You cannot claim any expenses that were or will be reimbursed by your employer. Keep a copy of Form T2200S or Form T2200, in case we ask to see it.

You need to meet all of the above conditions to be eligible to claim your home office expenses

You are eligible to claim working from home expenses

COVID-19 examples

Working more than 50% of the time from home for at least a month (four consecutive weeks) in 2020
Working from home for less than a month (four consecutive weeks) due to the COVID-19 pandemic
