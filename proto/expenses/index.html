Work space in the home - Employee expenses

As an employee, you may claim expenses you paid for the use of a work space in your home.

For your 2020 tax return, there are two options:

  • claim $2 for each day you worked from home within the eligibile period (up to a maximum of $400) due to the COVID-19 pandemic
    No receipts needed No work space calculation needed No T2200 form needed
    Refer to: Temporary flat rate method
  • claim the actual costs you incurred, supported by receipts and bills

If you are self-employed, refer to: Business-use-of-home expenses
