Assess your quarantine plan - Avoid contact

You selected that you:

  • have access to basic necessities for 14 days or longer
  • are sharing your place of quarantine with people not at risk

Will it be possible for you to avoid all contact with the people who didn't travel with you?

  • Yes, I can completely avoid having any contact
  • No, I will have some contact

Answering that you can avoid all contact means that you will:

  • stay in separate room(s), including a separate sleeping area
  • use a separate bathroom (if possible)
  • not be in the same room as those you did not travel with, including kitchens and shared living spaces
  • not need to use shared spaces of your hotel or building, like:
    • lobbies
    • restaurants
    • gyms or pools
    • communal hallways
