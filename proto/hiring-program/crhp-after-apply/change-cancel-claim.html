Claim period deadlines

There is a deadline to:

  • apply for the CRHP for each claim period, or
  • increase the amount of your previous claim

The deadline is 180 days after the end of the claim period.

There is no deadline to cancel or reduce the amount of a previous claim.

Change or cancel a claim

If you are changing a claim to apply for CEWS instead of CRHP for a claim period, you only need to apply for CEWS for the same period. Your CRHP application will be replaced when your CEWS claim is submitted.

Otherwise, the ability to change or cancel your CRHP claim online is not yet available.

If you need to make a correction to your CRHP claim, or cancel it, you will need to contact us.
