Periods you can apply for

How claim periods work

The claim periods for the hiring subsidy match the claim periods for the Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS). Because CRHP started June 6, 2021, the first period you can claim CRHP is claim period 17. Each claim period is a specific period of 4 weeks, beginning on a Sunday.

The subsidy does not renew automatically. Each period, you must confirm that you're eligible and calculate your amount according to that period's rules before you apply.

There is a deadline to apply or increase your claim for each period.
