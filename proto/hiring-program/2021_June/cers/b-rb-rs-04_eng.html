Get help for:Subsidy top-up

Lockdown support (subsidy top-up)

If one or more of your qualifying properties was subject to a public health restriction during the claim period, you may qualify for an additional subsidy. The top-up is 25% of qualifying rent expenses, pro-rated for the number of days in the 28-day period that the qualifying property was subject to the public health restriction. For more information on eligibility and to determine the subsidy top-up (if applicable), see Calculate your subsidy amount.
