Amount of non-cash gifts

Enter the amount of all non-cash gifts (also known as gifts in kind) the charity made to the organization. Amounts must be the fair market value of the gifts in Canadian dollars. Examples of non-cash gifts are gifts of property, such as land, artwork, equipment, securities, pharmaceuticals, and cultural and ecological property. For more information on fair market value, go to https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/charities-giving/charities/operating-a-registered-charity/issuing-receipts/determining-fair-market-value-gifts-kind-non-cash-gifts.htmlNote: this link will load in another window or tab
