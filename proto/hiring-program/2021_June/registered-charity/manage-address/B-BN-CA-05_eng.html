Address types

Your mailing address is the address the Canada Revenue Agency sends your business correspondence to.

Your physical address is the address at which the day-to-day activities of your business take place. The physical address must be the address of the physical location of the business. Rural routes and post office boxes cannot be used as a physical address.

Your books and records address is the address of the physical location where the business keeps its books and records.
