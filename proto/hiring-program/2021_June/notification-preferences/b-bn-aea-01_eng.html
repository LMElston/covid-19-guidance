New email address

You can add your own email address or an email address of another My Business Account user. To view the online mail, the other user must be an owner or an authorized representative for this business number.

Business owners, delegated authorities, and authorized representatives with level 2 authorization can register for online mail through My Business Account.

The email address must be in the proper format:

  • it must have one and only one @ symbol
  • it must not have a period next to the @ symbol
  • it must not start or end with a period
  • it must not have two periods next to each other
  • it must have at least one period after the @ symbol, but the period cannot be next to the @ symbol
  • it must have at least one character before and after the @ symbol
  • it must contain only characters, that is, A-Z, a-z, 0-9, -, _, ., or @

This table lists the accounts to select for online mail. There are three columns. The first column has a tick box to choose the account to associate with a new or existing email address. There is an option to select all accounts. The second column shows the account number and type. The third column shows the email addresses on file for the account.
