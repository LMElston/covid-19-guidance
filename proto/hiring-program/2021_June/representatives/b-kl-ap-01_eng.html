Levels of authorization

  • Level 1 / Niveau 1
    - Allow the Canada Revenue Agency to disclose information about the account(s).
  • Level 2 / Niveau 2
    - Allow the Canada Revenue Agency to disclose information about and accept changes to the account(s).
  • Level 3 / Niveau 3
    - Allow the Canada Revenue Agency to disclose information about and accept changes to the account(s) and allow this representative to add, change, or delete other representatives within their authority.
