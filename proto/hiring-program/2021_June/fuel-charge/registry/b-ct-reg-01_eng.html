Fuel Charge Registry

The registry makes it easy to confirm a fuel charge account’s registration type and status when a fuel delivery takes place. This is to make sure that a client is a registered person for purposes of the fuel charge and could be eligible for relief, at the time of delivery, by using an exemption certificate. Only accounts with an “Active” status on the date of delivery should be considered registered.

Before using registry

The registry will only show information for the following registration types:
  • registered specified air carriers;
  • registered specified marine carriers;
  • registered specified rail carriers;
  • registered distributors;
  • registered users of fuel;
  • registered emitters.
The Fuel Charge Registry is a free service. To use it, you need to input a fuel charge business number and a date of delivery.

Fuel charge business number

Enter the fuel charge account's business number, which is found on the completed Form L401, Fuel Charge Exemption Certificate.

Date of delivery

Enter the date of delivery to retrieve the registration type and status as of that date. The date entered cannot be in the future.
