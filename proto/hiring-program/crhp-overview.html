What time periods the subsidies cover

The Canada Recovery Hiring Program (CRHP) and Canada Emergency Wage Subsidy (CEWS) are both based on four-week claim periods. The two subsidies overlap for four of these claim periods, from June 6 to September 25, 2021 (periods 17 to 20). For these claim periods you can apply to either CRHP or CEWS, whichever you are eligible for and gives you the higher amount.

Who is eligible to apply

Most of the eligibility criteria for CRHP and CEWS are the same, including payroll account requirements and eligible revenue drops.

The same types of employers can apply for both CRHP and CEWS, except:

Type of employer Apply for
Publicly-listed eligible corporations Only CEWS
Eligible corporations that are not Canadian-controlled Only CEWS
Eligible Canadian-controlled private corporations (CCPCs) or cooperatives that would be considered CCPCs Either CEWS or CRHP
